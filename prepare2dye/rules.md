Welcome to Prepare2Dye! We hope you enjoy your stay here.  
However, there are rules you must follow while you are here.

1. <details><summary>No swearing; keep it clean</summary>

    ## No swearing; keep it clean

    You are not permitted to swear in any moderated channels. This also includes words that a moderator finds offensive, if that decision is found to be reasonable.

    If your message is not cleaned automatically, you are responsible for removing it yourself; or you will incur a penalty.

    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - First offence: formal warning
    - Further offences: mutes of increasing length, minimum 5 minutes.
    - Warnings and infractions for this rule, within reason, will be pardoned 168 hours (7 days) after being issued
    </details>
    </details>
2. <details><summary><i>Strictly</i> no Discord alts</summary>

    ## *Strictly* no Discord alts

    You are not permitted to have, in this server, alternative Discord accounts ('alts').

    If you are suspected to be in violation of this rule then a moderator may ask all accounts involved to present proof of account ownership. **Failure to provide proof of account ownership will result in all accounts involved being awarded an infraction, and will not protect them from future inquiry.**

    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - In any case of alternative accounts, the alt will be banned permanently.
    - When the infracting user is not currently banned, they will receive a formal warning.
    - When the infracting user *is* currently banned, including a temporary ban, both accounts are banned permanently.
    - If you waste the time of a member of staff, or falsely defend an alternate account, while they are attempting to deal with an alt, whether it is (suspected to be) your own or another's, then you will incur additional infractions under this rule.
    - After 3 infractions, you will be banned permanently.
    </details>
    </details>
3. <details><summary>You should put your friend codes for your systems in the relevant channels</summary>

    ## You should put your friend codes for your systems in the relevant channels

    There is no punishment for failure to do so, but it will reduce the ability of other players to play with you!
    </details>
4. <details><summary>Do not spam or flood channels</summary>

    ## Do not spam or flood channels

    You are not permitted to spam (send the same message repeatedly, or a single message in a large number of shorter messages, for example sending a sentence letter by letter), or flood (send a large number of attachments or messages that are otherwise extermely long) within moderated channels.

    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - Any infraction will result in a mute, the length of which is decided by the punishing moderator.
    </details>
    </details>
5. <details><summary>Be pleasant to everyone</summary>

    ## Be pleasant to everyone

    You must be polite to all members, even if you personally do not like them.

    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - First offence: formal warning
    - Further offences: mutes of increasing length, minimum 5 minutes.
    - Warnings and infractions for this rule, within reason, will be pardoned 168 hours (7 days) after being issued
    </details>
    </details>
6. <details><summary>Follow instructions given to you by staff</summary>

    ## Follow instructions given to you by staff

    If a member of staff gives you an instruction, then (within reason) you are expected to follow it.

    Staff members are under no obligation to justify to you their reasons when giving you instructions, and their decisions may be overturned only by:

    - Owner/co-owner decision
    - Majority staff vote

    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - Judgement on the severity of an awarded punishment is deferred to the member of staff who gave the instruction.
    </details>
    </details>
7. <details><summary>NSFW content is not tolerated anywhere on the server</summary>

    ## NSFW content is not tolerated anywhere on the server

    Posting NSFW content anywhere on the server will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
    </details>

8. <details><summary>Innuendo or other sexual content not already covered by rule 7 is prohibited also</summary>

    ## Innuendo or other sexual content not already covered by rule 7 is prohibited also

    Do not post innuendo or sexual content anywhere on the server.
    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - First offence: formal warning
    - Further offences: mutes of increasing length, minimum 5 minutes.
    - Warnings and infractions for this rule, within reason, will be pardoned 720 hours (30 days) after being issued
    </details>
9. <details><summary>Please keep conversations in English</summary>

    ## Please keep conversations in English

    This server is an English-only server; please do not use other languages.

    One or two phrases is ok, but don't overdo it.

    <details><summary>Infraction punishments</summary>

    ### Infraction punishments

    - First and second offences: formal warning
    - Further offences: mutes of increasing length, minimum 5 minutes.
    - Warnings and infractions for this rule, within reason, will be pardoned 12 hours after being issued
    </details>
    </details>
10. <details><summary>Any and all appeals must be sent through the appeals form</summary>

    ## Any and all appeals must be sent through the appeals form

    If you are banned, muted, or warned and you feel that this is unfair, submit an appeal through [the appeals form](https://prepare2dye.anvil.app/#appeal). ***If you attempt to message a member of staff directly, your appeal will be denied.***
    </details>
